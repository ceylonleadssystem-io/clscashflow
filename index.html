FAQ

Questions before you start?

Can I start without a card?+

Yes. Every plan starts with a 15-day free trial and no credit card required. When the trial ends, you can activate the plan through Payable.

Can I send invoices through WhatsApp?+

Yes. You can download the invoice PDF and share a clean WhatsApp message with the customer from the dashboard.

Do you support customers outside Sri Lanka?+

The system supports multi-currency workflows. Sri Lanka pricing is live first, and international pricing can be enabled as payment support expands.

What if I outgrow my plan?+

You can move from Solo to Studio or Business when your customer count, team size, or reporting needs grow. Your saved data stays with the account.
