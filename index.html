Common Questions

Everything you
need to know.

No jargon. No pressure. Just honest answers about how CeylonryLabs fits into your business — and whether it's the right step for you right now.

Do I need accounting knowledge to use this? +

No. CeylonryLabs is designed for business owners, not accountants. If you know your income, your expenses, and who owes you money — you can use the system from day one. We built it specifically so that no financial background is required.

Can I switch plans as my business grows? +

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Starter to Growth or Premium at any time, and all your existing data carries over seamlessly. We built the tiers to mirror the natural growth stages of a Sri Lankan SME, so upgrading feels like a natural progression, not a disruption.

Is my financial data secure and private? +

Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Only authorised users you assign can access your dashboard. We don't share, sell, or use your business data in any way — it belongs entirely to you and your company.

How long does it take to set up? +

Most businesses are fully set up and entering live transactions within a single day. Our onboarding is guided and simple — you'll walk away from your first session with a working dashboard and real numbers already inside the system.

Can I try it before committing to a plan? +

Yes — use the Live Demo button on this page to explore the full system with real sample data, no sign-up required. If you want to trial it with your own data, contact us and we'll set up a guided walkthrough session.

Does it work for multi-branch businesses? +

Multi-branch dashboards are available on the Premium plan. Each branch gets its own financial view, while you maintain a consolidated overview across all locations — a feature most enterprise tools charge a fortune for.

Is there a contract or lock-in period? +

No long-term contracts. We operate month to month, and you can cancel any time. We believe in earning your subscription every single month by being genuinely useful to your business — not by trapping you in a commitment.

I'm a busy mum. Honestly, how long will this take to learn? +

Most of our mum entrepreneurs are up and running in under 30 minutes. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use CeylonryLabs. We also include a free setup call where we walk you through everything — you don't have to figure it out alone. We designed this for women who are already doing too much, so we made it as simple as humanly possible.

What do I get during the 15-day free trial? +

Full access to your plan — financial dashboard, expense tracking, invoice management, payroll, who-owes-you tracking and business analytics — completely free for 15 days, with no credit card. You sign up online and you're inside in a few minutes; there's no waiting for a callback. When the 15 days are up, you simply choose to continue on a paid plan or stop.
