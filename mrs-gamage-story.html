The Story of Mrs. Gamage

Mrs. Gamage makes the best butter cake in Nugegoda.

Her customers love her. Orders come through WhatsApp. Friends recommend her. Birthdays, office treats, school events, family gatherings; everyone knows she is the person to call.

But behind the busy kitchen, there is another story.

Late payments. Untracked expenses. Receipts in drawers. Invoices sent manually. Money coming in, but disappearing just as quickly. A business that feels busy, but not always profitable.

Mrs. Gamage is not lazy.
She is not careless.
She is not lacking talent.

She simply never had a simple system that helped her see the truth of her business.

That is why Cashflow System was created.

To help business owners like Mrs. Gamage raise invoices, track payments, understand income, manage reports, and make better business decisions without feeling overwhelmed.

Because being busy is not the same as being profitable.

And every woman with a business deserves to know where her money is going.

Our Mission

Clarity, confidence, and structure.

Our mission is to help women entrepreneurs, mom business owners, and small SMEs take control of their business finances through simple, practical, and affordable digital tools.

We want to make invoicing, payment tracking, reports, customer management, and cashflow visibility easier for business owners who are building something meaningful with limited time, limited support, and real responsibilities.

Cashflow System exists to bring clarity, confidence, and structure to small businesses.

Our Vision

From survival mode to structure.

Our vision is to become the most trusted business control system for women-led and small businesses in Sri Lanka and beyond.

We believe thousands of talented women are running businesses from kitchens, homes, shops, salons, studios, and small offices, but many do not have access to the right systems, funding guidance, or business structure.

We want to help them grow from survival mode to confident, structured, profitable businesses.
